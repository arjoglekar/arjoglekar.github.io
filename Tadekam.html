Tadekam Foundation
Mahavtar Babaji Tadekam Foundation (MBTF) is a unique organization, probably the only one of its kind in the world. The visionary founder of MBTF along with his dedicated team bring simple result-oriented methods to people with the aim of ending the personal suffering and ignorance that humans dwell in and help them to lead lives of joy, excellence and illumination.
Tadekam means 'That One' which is the source of all that exists in the physical and spirit form. 'That One' energy which makes creation possible and 'That One' power that controls, sustains, destroys, and recreates all that is seen and unseen.
Our organization aims to bring about a greater awareness of your real connection to 'That One'. MBTF is not a cult, ashram, or new religion; nor does it subscribe to any organized religion, doctrine, sect, or belief system.
We offer programs that adopt new methods of operating ancient and traditional princilples of human enlightenment, keeping pace with new information and constantly evolving human race.
Our Founder
Guruji Naushir has been spreading the teachings of Mahavtar Babaji since the year 2000, including all the knowledge he has acquired from other Gurus. He founded Tadekam in 2007 to bring all the teaching and spiritual practices under one umbrella. He is in constant communication with Mahavtar Babaji whom he met in 2010.
Our Founder
Guruji Naushir, a successful professional, was a man of the world, till he met with three sages between 1998 and 2003. This propelled him to take a journey 'within', and from his intense seeking came answers to a human being's deep rooted sufferings and miseries.
His deep compassion compelled him to dedicate his life to serving humanity, and he founded Mahavatar Babaji Tadekam Foundation (MBTF) with the motto – From illness to wellness, from suffering to solution and from ignorance to enlightenment.
Mahavtar Babaji, the Omnipresent Guru, who is believed to be over 2000 years old, first started communicating with Guruji Naushir in 2000 leading to his meeting Babaji in person in 2010. In the course of these many years, Mahavtar Babaji, who is also made famous by Paramahansa Yogananda in his book 'Autobiography of a Yogi', has communicated certain spiritual information to Guruji Naushir which is designed into Programs and courses created and conducted under MBTF.

I would like to share the story of his spiritual journey with you and the incredibly close bond he has with his mentor, his God and his Guru….. Team Tadekam
The balance sheet of Tadekam has only one asset. The asset comprises of the small but growing number of deeply committed individuals who train and work under the Tadekam banner. The collective aim of every individual associated with Tadekam is common. That is to accomplish the vision spelt out in the mission statement.
What is unique is that all teachers, organizers, coordinators and supporters, work for the organization without any agenda of profit or personal recognition. Their motive is completely altruistic and no effort is spared to fulfill the organization's primary goal of taking humanity from suffering and ignorance to enlightenment.
All Tadekam Teachers hold the license to teach the various courses and the programs as designed by the founder Guruji Naushir.
